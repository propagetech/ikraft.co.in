IKRAFT is one of the leading interior solutions companies in India offering a gamut of comprehensive and integrated spatial solutions across a range of verticals such as Corporate, Residential, Healthcare, Hospitality, Retail, Institutional, etc.

OUR APPROACH

General Contracting
As an experienced general contractor, with a dynamic estimating team, having best practices in procurement, ensuring long-standing relationships with local trade contractors, professional project management & skilled site staff; IKRAFT has a reputation for delivering competitively priced projects on time. We always ensure that safety, quality and a comprehensive approach to risk management are the core values of our projects.

Design – Build
We at IKRAFT provide a one-stop shop for project delivery. From concept to completion, we handle every single detail of a project for you. With decades of expertise, established relationships with highly qualified consulting and trading partners, we not only conceptualise your vision but also build it for you.

Turnkey fit-out Solutions
Balancing the creativity of our talented design & execution teams with astute budgetary oversight & timely deliveries, we at IKRAFT offer complete turn-key fitout solutions. Not only do we bring in elegant design & quality craftsmanship but also seamlessly managing the whole project for you.

Project Management
IKRAFT’S collaborative Project Management approach unites owners, architects and sub-contractors for speed, efficiency and accuracy. Acting on the owner’s behalf, our team manages the Pre-construction, Construction and Post-Construction process to ensure superior results. Our expertise adds value to critical areas of planning, design, budgeting, value engineering, logistical planning, cost control, scheduling, safety, and site management.

IN-HOUSE MODULAR FURNITURE FACTORY
IKRAFT’s state-of-art manufacturing facility located in Hyderabad is equipped with efficient machinery coupled with our highly skilled artisans in the workforce to produce high quality, cost-effective, tailor made products to suit every project need.
