About Us

IKRAFT is a well-established interior contracting firm offering a comprehensive & integrated gamut of interior solutions ranging from General Contracting, Turnkey Fitout solutions, Project Management & Design Build.

With decades of knowledge and experience stitched into the fabric of our workforce, our dedicated team at IKRAFT work tirelessly to tailor-make every project to suit the client’s requirement whilst never compromising on the quality of the fundamental components of design.

We are recognised for our high quality workmanship, innovative solutions and a fine-tuned ability to fast-track projects. Our biggest strength is our ability to quickly mobilize resources and effectively manage projects to the highest quality standards and the shortest schedules.

Lastly we believe in approaching every single project with a fresh and unbiased perspective and make sure that what we deliver is absolutely in sync with our client’s vision & narrative.

OUR VISION

To be a reputable, sustainable and desirable turnkey contracting firm of global standards, providing state of the art ingenious build with superior quality.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create significant value for our clients and teams through contentious improvement, creativity & innovation towards our pursuit of excellence.

OUR VALUES

EXCELLENCE: We have an uncompromising commitment to excellence on all fronts. We strive to provide our customers with the best possible solutions and work towards a flawless customer experience.

COLLABORATION: We work together, building strong partnerships and relationships with our colleagues, as well as with our clients.

COMMITMENT: We are proactive in finding the right fit out solutions for our clients and remain committed to our work at all times.

RESPECT: Respect is a guiding value for us; we respect differences, diverse opinions and value contributions, inputs shared by our customers and our team.

INTEGRITY: Acting ethically and transparently in all business dealings, prioritizing doing the right thing over personal gain. Being accountable and following through with decisions that have been made.
